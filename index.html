Ulukmanapo

Ulukmanapo is a young and talented rapper. The singer believes that music is not just a means of income for him, but a whole way of life into which he invests himself.
The hip-hop star was born and raised in Kyrgyzstan. Kyrgyz by nationality. Born on September 18, 1993 in Bishkek, in a family of intellectuals.
Ulukmanapo talks little about his childhood and parents. He began to study music during his school years; some of his favorite performers are Snoop Dogg and Drake. In his free time, he began writing songs, sharing them with his friends. They, in turn, supported the guy, convincing him that he had a talent for writing texts, so he should try himself in this area without missing out on the opportunity. The future performer was not particularly inspired by this idea of ​​music, because he does not have the appropriate education, equipment, or connections, thanks to which many make their way into the world. But he decided to try the coffee maker on social media and streaming tables first.
Since 2016, the singer has already begun to delight listeners with the results of his work. Most of all, the songwriter tried to realize himself on YouTube, and he was right. His videos gained immense popularity and began to gain millions of views. As a result, in 2019, his composition “Not Today / Seventy-Fifth” received a gold certificate. This video has received almost 3 million views. The young performer admits that he could hardly believe such a resounding success.
Ulukmanapo's work harmoniously combines aggressiveness, tenderness and melody. The young man writes his songs with soul. Many people appreciate the creativity of Ulukmanapo for its so-called boyish theme. The musician draws the lyrics for his songs, one might say, from his life. The people who surround the musician, the events taking place around him, all this forms the basis of his tracks.
